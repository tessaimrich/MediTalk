The usual dose of Amlodipine/Valsartan Mylan is one tablet per day.

It is preferable to take your medicine at the same time each day.

Swallow the tablets with a glass of water.

You can take Amlodipine/Valsartan Mylan with or without food.

Do not take Amlodipine/Valsartan Mylan with grapefruit or grapefruit juice.
